Search engine giant Google on Wednesday announced the first batch of ten digital news start-ups
that will form the cohort of its GNI Startups Lab.Chosen out of over 70 applicants from across India,
the ten news start-ups span a diverse array of journalism, including investigative, broadcast,political,
data and local news organisations that give a voice to underrepresented communities. The diverse cohort
features newsrooms from around the country, producing news in languages including English, Hindi, Malayalam
and Urdu, Google said in a statement.
